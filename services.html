Compliance, GDPR & Risk

If you don’t understand your data, you can’t comply with GDPR.

Right to be forgotten is simple in principle — but difficult in reality. If you don’t know where data exists, how it is duplicated, or how it flows between systems, you cannot reliably delete it.

VIZIQ supports the data foundations required for effective compliance and auditability.
